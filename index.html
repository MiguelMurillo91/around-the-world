Who we are

We are a group of international students at George brown college in the sports and event marketing postgraduate program. We came from India, China, Brazil, and Colombia! What a wonderful opportunity to share and learn from our cultures through the Multicultural Festival! We look to provide you an immersive cultural experience in the form of being able to be at multiple places at the same time!

Charity

Around the World strongly stands by support and outreach for YMCA of Greater Toronto as our official charity partner.
