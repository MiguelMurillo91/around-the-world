Welcome to Around the World: A Multicultural Festival!

Join us to a celebration of global unity, diversity, and cultural exchange where inclusivity and diversification take centre stage! We will explore food booths and delighting in typical dishes from diverse countries such as India, China, Mexico, Japan, Spain, Brazil, Colombia, and more!

Who we are?

We are Global Fusion Crew! A group of international students at George Brown College in the Sports and Event Marketing postgraduate program. We came from India, China, Brazil, and Colombia! What a wonderful opportunity to share and learn from our cultures through Around the World: a Multicultural Festival! We look to provide you an immersive cultural experience in the form of being able to be at multiple places at the same time!

Our Charity

Around the World: A Multicultural Festival emphasizes the richness of diversity while highlighting the strength that comes from different cultures coming together for a shared cause. It encapsulates the essence of a multicultural festival aimed at fundraising for the YMCA of Greater Toronto.
